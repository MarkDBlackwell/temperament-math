A range -2..2 of fifths produces:

* 2 sets of thirds, rising to
      G D A E B F# C# G# D# A# F C

* 3 sets of fifths, also rising to
      G D A E B F# C# G# D# A# F C

* Corresponding accumulated fifths, rising to
      G D A E B F# C# G# D# A# F C

* Corresponding reordered stepwise notes, indicating the positions of
      C# D D# E F F# G G# A A# B C

* And corresponding tuning sets, also indicating the positions of
      C# D D# E F F# G G# A A# B C
